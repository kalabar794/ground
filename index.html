5 things you can see
Look around and name five things in view. Let your eyes move slowly.
4 things you can touch
Find four surfaces. Press your hand into each one long enough to notice texture and temperature.
3 things you can hear
Pause. Catch three separate sounds, near or far.
2 things you can smell
Notice two scents in the air, on your clothes, or nearby objects.
1 thing you can taste
Notice the taste in your mouth or take a sip of water and focus on it.
